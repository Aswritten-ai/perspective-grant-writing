## 2025-06-12: Strategy shift for computational biology proposals

The project has integrated a new strategic framework for computational biology grant writing, moving away from "novel algorithm" framing toward biological problem-solving. This shift addresses a settled decision to prioritize biological impact over technical novelty to overcome structural biases in wet-lab review panels.
